Weeknight Lemon Garlic Pasta

Serves 4 · 20 minutes · One pan

A bright, fast pasta for the end of a long day. This page exists to demonstrate a rapid web-page prototype shipped through CI/CD to Azure Static Web Apps — edit the HTML, push to main, and the change is live in about a minute.

  • Prep5 min
  • Cook15 min
  • Serves4

Ingredients

  • 340 g spaghetti or linguine
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 lemon — zest and juice
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 40 g parmesan, finely grated, plus more to serve
  • A handful of parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Method

  1. Boil the pasta in well-salted water until al dente. Reserve a cup of the cooking water before draining.
  2. While it cooks, warm the olive oil over medium-low heat and gently fry the garlic and red pepper flakes until fragrant and just golden — do not let the garlic brown.
  3. Add the lemon zest and juice to the pan, then the drained pasta. Toss to coat.
  4. Add a splash of the reserved pasta water and the parmesan, tossing until a light glossy sauce forms.
  5. Finish with parsley, a crack of black pepper, and extra parmesan. Serve immediately.
